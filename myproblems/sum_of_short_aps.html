Sum of Short Arithmetic Sequences

input

N M
l_1 r_1 a_1 b_1
l_2 r_2 a_2 b_2
...
l_M r_M a_M b_M

constraints

  • for all i(1 ≤ i ≤ M),
    • 1 ≤ li ≤ ri ≤ n
    • 0 ≤ ai, bi ≤ 109
    • 1 ≤ n ≤ 105
    • 1 ≤ m ≤ 105

problem

  • An arithmetic sequence ci(n) is defined as follows:
    • ci(n) = ai + (n − li)bi if li ≤ n ≤ ri;
    • otherwise, ci = 0.
  • Answer $d_n := \sum_{i=1}^{M}c_i(n)$ for 1 ≤ n ≤ N.

output

d_1 d_2 ... d_n

sample input 1

10 3
5 6 1 9
1 4 3 5
2 9 5 1

sample output 1

3 13 19 25 9 19 10 11 12 0
